We often carry an overconfidence — or sometimes a completely misplaced confidence — in our knowledge and capabilities. We build our worldview, our opinions, and even our predictions based on that confidence, without checking if our assumptions are actually right or wrong.

From my own experience, this is one of the biggest contributors to failure.

Feeling good about ourselves is one thing.  
Doing something genuinely impactful is something completely different.
