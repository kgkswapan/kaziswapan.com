## Sections

1. **Timeline** – minute-by-minute from detection to resolution.
2. **Guardrails** – controls that failed or were missing.
3. **Detection quality** – did alerts trigger? was noise a factor?
4. **Remediation** – code, infra, or procedural changes with owners/dates.
